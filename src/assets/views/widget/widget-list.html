Head 1

The new Fire TV works a lot like Google’s $70 Chromecast Ultra. The Amazon competitor is neat little dongle that comes with 4K HDR at 60 fps, Dolby Atmos support, a 1.5 Ghz quad-core processor, and 802.11ac wi-fi. The small square dongle also plugs right into your TV, so it can effectively disappear from view. There’s also a regular Alexa Voice Remote in case you want to talk to your Fire TV or smash buttons like a caveman.
